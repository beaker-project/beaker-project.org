Pre-built packages

Server packages are available via yum for Red Hat Enterprise Linux 6 and 7 (and compatible distros) and Fedora 20+:

beaker-server-RedHatEnterpriseLinux.repo
beaker-server-Fedora.repo

Client packages are available via yum for RHEL6, RHEL7 (and corresponding CentOS releases) and Fedora:

beaker-client-RedHatEnterpriseLinux.repo
beaker-client-CentOS.repo
beaker-client-Fedora.repo
